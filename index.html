What is this?

      Maze 95 JS or maze95-js is a recreation of the Windows 95 3D Maze screensaver in the form of a playable web game.
      I am planning to add online multiplayer functionality in the future once the game is in a complete enough state, you 
      also may have noticed the disabled "Story Mode" button on the new Title Screen, this hints at there being a gamemode 
      where there is an objective to follow instead of roaming around the maze.
